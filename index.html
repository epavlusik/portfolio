About Me

Creating websites is activity, where I can express my inner artist. The other important fact about me is that I am perfectionist when it comes to my work. I am willing to learn and to become better at what I enjoy to do. In terms of cooperation I am friendly and I have deep respect for diversity of human souls and opinions.

Project 1: Personal Website

This website is using only HTML5 and CSS3. It is my personal website presenting my older programming projects and art. It was also first website that was hosted on Github pages and connected with domain.

Project 2: Above Us - Graphic novel

I had vision about transforming my graphic novel into digital form with interactive gui. I used canvas element that offers many interesting possibilities. For effective displaying of text-bubbles I used parsed JSON object.
